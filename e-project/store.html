About Us


At PuppyCo, we pride ourselves on being far more than just a service. We're committed to placing healthy puppies into happy homes and have a number of measures in place to ensure the health and happiness of your new family member. One of them is our Health Guarantee, which is only activated once you take your puppy to a licensed veterinarian within three (3) business days of arrival of your puppy.
